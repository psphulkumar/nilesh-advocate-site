About Advocate Nilesh Kumar

Professional Background

Advocate Nilesh Kumar is a dedicated and experienced legal professional serving clients in Begusarai and surrounding areas. He practices in the Begusarai District Court, providing expert legal counsel and representation.

Education

Nilesh Kumar holds a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) degree from the Central University of South Bihar (CUSB) in Gaya. He completed the five-year integrated LLB program, equipping him with comprehensive knowledge of Indian legal system and jurisprudence.

Practice Areas

With a strong focus on delivering ethical and efficient legal services, Advocate Nilesh Kumar handles a wide range of cases, including civil matters, criminal defense, family law disputes, property-related issues, consumer matters, labour disputes and other legal concerns. Advocate Nilesh Kumar is a legal professional offering expert legal services and is a renowned RTI activist and a famous labour law expert associated with Begusarai Court. He has extensive experience handling labour law and consumer cases.

Commitment to Clients

Advocate Nilesh Kumar is committed to providing personalized legal solutions, ensuring that each client receives dedicated attention and professional representation. He believes in maintaining the highest standards of legal ethics and client confidentiality.
